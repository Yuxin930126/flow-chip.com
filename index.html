About Flowchip

We are committed to becoming a globally leading microfluidics technology-driven enterprise. With advanced chip design and manufacturing as the core, we empower cutting-edge research and industrial applications in fluid mechanics, biological detection, energy and environment, and other fields.

Through an open, precise, and efficient microfluidics platform, we hope to break down the barriers between experiments and applications and promote the leap from basic science to engineering practice.

Taking innovation as the engine, reliability as the foundation, and sustainable development as the goal, we serve industries such as oil and gas, chemical, pharmaceutical, agricultural, food, and environment, and contribute to human clean energy, healthy life, and sustainable future.
